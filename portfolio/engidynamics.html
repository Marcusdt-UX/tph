Client Project

EngiDynamics Consulting

Engineering Consultancy • Rochester, MI

A professional single-page website for an engineering and design consultancy — delivered through the Port Huron EDA grant program.

Project Overview

EngiDynamics Consulting needed a professional web presence to showcase their engineering and design services. As part of the Port Huron EDA grant program, we delivered a clean, effective marketing site on a lean budget — proving you don't need a massive investment to look professional online.

The single-page WordPress site highlights their six core services, team expertise, and makes it easy for prospects to get in touch — exactly what an engineering consultancy needs.

Single Page WordPress Responsive Design EDA Grant

What We Delivered

Key deliverables for the EngiDynamics project

Single-Page Design

A streamlined, scroll-based layout that presents all essential information — services, team bios, and contact — without navigation confusion.

Responsive Build

Fully responsive WordPress theme that looks crisp and professional on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

Service Showcase

Clean presentation of their six core engineering services with clear descriptions that help prospects understand exactly what EngiDynamics offers.

The Challenge

Deliver a professional, credible web presence for an engineering consultancy on a limited EDA grant budget — without cutting corners on design quality or brand perception.

Our Solution

We chose a single-page WordPress approach — maximizing impact with minimal page count. Clean typography, professional imagery, and strategic content placement create a high-value impression without complex development.
