Client Project

Four Farms Creations

Custom Apparel & E-Commerce

A full WooCommerce storefront for a handcrafted custom goods brand — from product catalog to checkout, with branding that captures the brand's rustic farmstead personality.

Project Overview

Four Farms Creations specializes in handcrafted custom t-shirts, tumblers, and mugs with countryside-inspired designs. They needed more than a simple website — they needed a full e-commerce platform that could handle product variations, cart management, and secure checkout.

We built their store on WordPress with WooCommerce, creating a visual identity that reflects the brand's rustic, farm-inspired aesthetic while making the shopping experience smooth and intuitive.

WordPress WooCommerce E-Commerce Branding

What We Delivered

A complete e-commerce solution from brand identity to launch

WooCommerce Store

Full product catalog with variations (sizes, colors), cart, checkout, and order management — everything needed to sell online.

Brand Identity

Visual design that captures the rustic farmstead personality — warm colors, handcrafted feel, and countryside-inspired imagery throughout.

Payment Integration

Secure payment processing with multiple payment options, giving customers a seamless and trusted checkout experience.

The Challenge

Transform a small handcrafted goods business into a professional e-commerce brand — with product photography presentation, inventory management, and a checkout flow that builds trust with first-time buyers.

Our Solution

Built on WooCommerce for easy self-management, with a custom theme that showcases products beautifully. Streamlined the cart-to-checkout flow and added trust signals throughout — making it easy for customers to buy with confidence.
