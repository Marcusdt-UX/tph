anke — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 July 2026

anke is a private lending-circle and personal-finance app operated by Marcus Thomas ("we"). This policy explains what we collect, why, and the choices you have. anke is currently in a closed friends-and-family beta in the United States.

What we collect

  • Account details — your name and email address, used to sign you in and identify you to other members of circles you join.
  • Lending activity — circles you create or join, contributions, loans, votes, and repayments you record in the app.
  • Wallet data — your blockchain wallet address and USDC balance on the Base network. Wallets are created and operated for you by Circle Internet Financial; anke never holds your private keys.
  • Linked bank data (optional) — if you connect a bank through our data provider (SimpleFIN), we receive read-only account balances and transaction history to power budgeting and cash-flow features. We never receive your bank login credentials.
  • Financial details you enter — budgets, goals, debts, and loans you add manually.
  • Push notification token — so we can alert you about votes, payments, and money arriving. You can disable notifications in your device settings.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell or rent your personal or financial data to anyone.
  • We do not show third-party advertising.
  • We do not collect or store bank passwords, card numbers, or wallet private keys. Purchases of USDC are handled entirely by Coinbase; bank connections by SimpleFIN.

How your data is used

Solely to provide the app: run your circles and lending book, keep your balance and transactions accurate, sync linked bank data you asked us to connect, send you relevant notifications, and improve reliability and security. The optional in-app assistant ("Ask anke") sends a summary of your own financial figures to an AI model provider to answer your questions; it is used only to generate your answer.

Who we share it with

We share data only with the service providers that make the app work: Supabase (secure database and backend hosting), Circle Internet Financial (wallet and USDC infrastructure), Coinbase (adding money), SimpleFIN (bank data you connect), Expo (push notifications), and our AI model provider (only when you use Ask anke). Each processes data only to deliver its part of the service. We may disclose information if required by law.

Data retention & deletion

We keep your data while your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated personal data at any time by emailing us at the address below; we will delete it except where we must retain records to comply with law. On-chain transactions recorded on the public Base network cannot be deleted.

Security

Data is protected with encryption in transit, row-level access controls so you can only see your own and your circle members' relevant data, and server-side controls on all money movement. No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

Children

anke is not directed to anyone under 18 and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be noted by updating the date above.
