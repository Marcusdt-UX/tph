anke

Lending circles for family & friends

Pool money, help each other out, and keep it all honest.

Private beta

anke brings the age-old savings circle into an app. A small group — your family, your friends — pools money together, and members can borrow from the pot and pay it back on agreed terms. anke keeps the books, handles the votes, and makes sure every dollar is accounted for.

What you can do

👥

Shared circles

Start a circle with people you trust. Everyone contributes, anyone can request a loan, and the group approves it together.

📒

Personal lending book

Lent cash to someone the old-fashioned way? Track it, set the terms, and send a simple link so they can pay you back.

💰

Real wallet

Add money, send it to a circle member, or cash out — in plain dollars, with the crypto plumbing kept out of your way.

📈

Earn on idle funds

A circle can put money that isn't loaned out to work, so the pot grows a little while it waits.

Every dollar is really there. Money in an anke circle is held as USDC — a digital dollar worth exactly $1 — on a public network, so balances can't be faked and the books always match what's actually in the pot.

How it works

  1. Create a circle or start your personal lending book — one tap, no paperwork.
  2. Add money and contribute to the pot; invite the people you trust.
  3. Request a loan, vote to approve, and pay it back on the terms you set — anke tracks every step.

Who it's for

Families and close-knit groups who already lend and borrow among themselves — and want it organized, fair, and clear, without awkward conversations about who owes what.
