Delete your anke account & data

anke, by Thomas Publishing House (Marcus Thomas) · Last updated 12 July 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your anke account and the data associated with it.

How to request deletion

Email info@thomaspublishinghouse.com from the email address on your anke account, with the subject "Delete my anke account."

We verify the request comes from your account email and process it within 30 days. We'll confirm by email when it's done.

What gets deleted

  • Your profile — name and email address
  • Your wallet records in anke and your app balance history
  • Your circle memberships, contributions, loans, votes, and personal lending-book entries
  • Your budgets, goals, and any linked-bank connection and transaction data
  • Your push-notification token

What may be kept

  • Records we must keep by law (for example, minimal transaction records for legal or accounting requirements), retained only as long as required, then deleted.
  • Public blockchain transactions. anke wallets settle in USDC on the Base network. Transactions recorded on that public blockchain are permanent and cannot be deleted by anyone, including us.
  • Shared circle activity. If you were part of a shared circle, records needed for the remaining members' accurate history (for example, that a repaid loan occurred) may be retained without your personal identifying details.

Deleting only some of your data

You don't have to delete your whole account to remove specific data. Email the address above describing what you'd like removed — for example disconnecting a linked bank, or deleting specific budget or loan entries — and we'll handle it within 30 days.

Questions
