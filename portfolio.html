Leading Light

Startup UX — Concept to Prototype

Safety & Navigation

Led UX and front-end development for a safety-focused navigation prototype that prioritized emotional design, community reporting, and lean development.

Traditional navigation tools ignore how users feel while traveling. For many — especially vulnerable commuters — this creates stress and unsafe workarounds.

Our Solution

Conducted 10+ user interviews, built journey maps around emotional decision-making, designed danger zone visualizations and one-tap emergency messaging, and built the prototype in React.
