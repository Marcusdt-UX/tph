Little Caesars

Fundraising Platform UX Redesign

Enterprise & Fundraising

Case Study 02 2025

Consumers Energy

Internal Utility Planning App UX

Utility & Enterprise

Redesigned a GIS-based planning tool to improve usability, reduce training time, and streamline operations across forestry and field teams.

The Challenge

An existing process relying on spreadsheets and technical tools, making it difficult for newer or non-technical planners to step in without significant training.

Our Solution

Conducted user interviews and card-sorting, created personas, designed an intuitive map-based dashboard with context-aware tooltips and guided planning paths.

Case Study 03 2024

Leading Light

Startup UX — Concept to Prototype

Safety & Navigation

Led UX and front-end development for a safety-focused navigation prototype that prioritized emotional design, community reporting, and lean development.

The Challenge

Traditional navigation tools ignore how users feel while traveling. For many — especially vulnerable commuters — this creates stress and unsafe workarounds.

Our Solution

Conducted 10+ user interviews, built journey maps around emotional decision-making, designed danger zone visualizations and one-tap emergency messaging, and built the prototype in React.
