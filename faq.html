Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, process, and pricing. Can't find what you're looking for? We're here to help.

Starting your journey is simple. First, schedule a free consultation through our contact page. We'll discuss your goals, challenges, and vision. Then, we'll provide a custom proposal outlining our recommended strategy, timeline, and investment. Once approved, we'll kick off with a discovery session to dive deep into your brand and begin crafting your digital presence.

We'll need details about your business goals, target audience, brand assets (logos, colors, fonts), existing marketing materials, competitor examples you admire, and any specific features or functionality you require. Don't worry if you don't have everything—we can help you develop what's missing as part of our process.

Timeline varies by complexity. A standard 5-page website typically takes 4-6 weeks from kickoff to launch. More complex projects with custom features, e-commerce, or extensive content can take 8-12 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline in your proposal and keep you updated throughout the process with regular milestones.

We also provide training so you can manage day-to-day updates yourself if preferred. Many clients opt for ongoing retainers to continue growing their digital presence.

We craft compelling copy that tells your brand story, optimizes for search engines, and converts visitors into customers. This includes website copy, blog articles, email campaigns, social media content, and editorial strategy.

Definitely. While we're proudly based in Port Huron, Michigan, we serve clients nationwide and internationally. All our services are available remotely through video calls, project management tools, and digital collaboration. We've successfully worked with clients from coast to coast.

Project-based pricing is a one-time investment for specific deliverables (like building a website). Monthly retainers provide ongoing services like content creation, SEO, social media management, and continuous optimization. Many clients start with a project to build their foundation, then continue with a retainer for growth.

We typically structure payments in milestones: 50% to begin work, 25% at the midpoint, and 25% upon completion. For monthly retainers, payment is due at the beginning of each month. We accept credit cards, ACH transfers, and checks. Custom payment arrangements can be discussed for larger projects.

We believe in transparent pricing. Our proposals clearly outline all costs. The only additional expenses would be third-party services you choose (like premium hosting, stock photography, or paid advertising budgets), which we'll discuss and approve with you beforehand. No surprises.

We specialize in modern web technologies including React, TypeScript, Next.js, and Tailwind CSS for custom development, with deep expertise in UX design tools like Figma for wireframing, prototyping, and design systems. For content-managed sites, we work with WordPress, Shopify, and Webflow. We choose the best platform based on your specific needs, budget, and technical comfort level.

Yes, absolutely. Upon final payment, you own all custom code, designs, and content we create for you. We'll provide you with all source files and credentials. Your website is your asset. We simply ask for permission to showcase our work in our portfolio (unless you prefer to remain confidential).

Every website we create is fully responsive and optimized for all devices—smartphones, tablets, laptops, and desktops. With over 60% of web traffic coming from mobile devices, mobile-first design is standard in all our projects, not an add-on.

SEO is built into our process from day one. We conduct keyword research, optimize site structure and content, ensure technical best practices (fast loading, clean code, proper meta tags), and create a content strategy. Our Manuscript and First Edition packages include ongoing SEO with regular content creation, performance tracking, and UX-informed improvements to boost search rankings.

Results vary by industry and goals, but our clients typically see significant improvements within 3-6 months. Our case studies — including work for brands like Little Caesars and Consumers Energy — show measurable improvements in completion rates, mobile engagement, and planning efficiency. We set realistic expectations and track metrics that matter to your business.

We establish clear KPIs during our discovery phase based on your goals. This might include website traffic, conversion rates, lead generation, search rankings, social engagement, or revenue. We provide regular reports showing progress and adjust strategies based on data to continuously improve performance.
